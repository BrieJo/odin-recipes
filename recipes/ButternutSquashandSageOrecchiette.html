Butternut Squash and Sage Orecchiette

Description

A delicious vegetarian pasta with butternut squash and mushrooms as the focal point of the dish. Great for the autumn season and serves 2 people. Double the ingredients when you have more guests coming over.

Ingredients

  • orecchiette pasta
  • butternut squash (8 ounce)
  • 1 shallot
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1/4 sage
  • 4 ounce button mushrooms
  • chili flakes (1 tsp)
  • parmesan cheese
  • olive oil (2 tsp)
  • butter (3 tbsp)
  • pepper
  • 1/4 tsp sugar
  • kosher salt

Steps

  1. Bring 8 cups salted water to boil (add a bit more salt then you normally would, you'll need it for later)
  2. Add pasta and cook 15-17 minutes al dente. Wash and dry all produce while it's cooking
  3. Halve, peel and thinly slice shallot. Roughly chop sage until you have 1 TBSP. Thinly slice mushrooms. Finely chop garlic.
  4. While pasta cooks, in microwave safe bowl, combine butternut squash and a splash of water. Cover with plastic and poke a few holes in wrap microwave until tender. About two minutes, drain if necessary.
  5. Heat a large drizzle of olive oil in a large pan over medium high heat. Add butternut squash and 1/4 tsp sugar. Cook, strring occasionally, until browned and slightly crispy, 2-3 minutes.
  6. Add shallot and chopped sage. Cook, stirring, until lightly browned, 1-2 minutes. Add a pinch of chili flakes; season with salt.
  7. Add mushrooms to pan and cook, stirring occasionally, until lightly browned, about 3 minutes. Add garlic and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute. Season with salt and pepper.
  8. Add pasta and 3 TBSP butter to pan; stir until butter has melted. Add reserved pasta, cooking water and half the parmesan; stir until thoroughly combined. Season with salt and pepper.
  9. Split and sprinkle with remaining parmesan cheese and chili flakes.
